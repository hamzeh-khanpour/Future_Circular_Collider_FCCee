OVERALL SCIENTIFIC GOAL OF THE 15 SLIDES
----------------------------------------
The 15 slides must answer, in a coherent story, the following physics questions:

1. Why is FCC-ee a precision machine rather than only a Higgs factory?
2. Why does the FCC programme begin with e+e− before going to FCC-hh?
3. What are the FCC-ee running stages: Z pole, WW threshold, ZH Higgs stage, and top threshold/365 GeV?
4. Why does FCC-ee provide exceptional precision?
   - clean e+e− initial state,
   - known centre-of-mass energy,
   - very large luminosity,
   - four interaction points,
   - low pile-up,
   - full-event reconstruction,
   - resonant depolarisation and beam-energy calibration,
   - high-quality detector environment.
5. What does the Z-pole programme measure?
   - Z mass and width,
   - line shape,
   - forward-backward asymmetries,
   - effective weak mixing angle,
   - Rb, Rc, heavy-flavour EW observables,
   - tau observables,
   - αQED(mZ), αS(mZ), Nν,
   - rare and forbidden Z decays.
6. What does the WW-threshold programme measure?
   - W mass,
   - W width,
   - WW cross section,
   - diboson dynamics,
   - anomalous triple-gauge couplings,
   - constraints relevant to SMEFT fits.
7. Why are precision Z and WW measurements essential for precision Higgs physics?
   - Z-pole data determine Zee and other electroweak couplings.
   - The Zee vertex enters e+e− → ZH.
   - Without improved EW inputs, uncertainties in EW couplings would contaminate Higgs coupling extraction.
   - In SMEFT, gauge invariance links modifications in Higgs couplings, electroweak vertices, and triple gauge couplings.
   - Z-pole data reduce correlations between Higgs and EW sectors.
   - Diboson/WW data constrain aTGCs such as δg1,Z and δκγ, which are correlated with HVV interactions.
8. Why is the ZH stage at √s ≈ 240 GeV central?
   - It is near the maximum of the ZH production cross section.
   - It enables the recoil-mass method.
   - The inclusive ZH cross section provides a model-independent anchor for κZ.
   - It provides absolute normalisation for other Higgs couplings.
   - It enables searches for invisible and exotic Higgs decays.
9. How are Higgs couplings extracted?
   - Through inclusive σ(ZH), exclusive σ×BR channels, H→bb, cc, gg, ττ, WW*, ZZ*, γγ, Zγ, μμ, invisible, untagged modes.
   - Through global κ fits and SMEFT fits.
   - Through combination of 240 GeV ZH and higher-energy WW-fusion / ννH information.
   - Through total width extraction using absolute production normalisation and branching-ratio information.
10. Why is this competitive with HL-LHC?
   - FCC-ee provides model-independent absolute coupling measurements; hadron colliders often need assumptions such as |κV|≤1 for width-related fits.
   - FCC-ee reaches sub-percent and per-mille-level precision in key couplings such as κZ and κW.
   - FCC-ee provides strong invisible/exotic sensitivity through recoil and full-event reconstruction.
   - HL-LHC is powerful but systematics/theory/backgrounds and lack of direct width access limit some measurements.
11. Why is the 365 GeV/top stage important for Higgs and EW physics?
   - Adds WW-fusion sensitivity to κW and ΓH.
   - Improves Higgs coupling correlations.
   - Provides access to top mass, top width, top electroweak couplings, and indirect top Yukawa sensitivity.
   - The ZH cross-section dependence on √s adds sensitivity to the Higgs self-coupling in global fits.
12. How does FCC-ee precision connect to FCC-hh?
   - FCC-ee supplies absolute normalisation, clean electroweak inputs, and model-independent Higgs constraints.
   - FCC-hh adds enormous Higgs yield, rare Higgs modes, top Yukawa, double-Higgs production, and direct high-mass reach.
   - The two stages are complementary: precision first, energy frontier next.
13. How does this precision programme motivate the later top-FCNC seminar?
   - Rare top interactions are another example of tiny SM effects magnified by clean conditions and precision reconstruction.
   - The FCNC study should be presented as part of the broader FCC-ee precision and rare-process programme, not as an isolated analysis.
